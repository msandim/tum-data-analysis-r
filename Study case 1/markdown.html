4. Does the genotype also show differences in other media?

In order to analyze the growth differences in the several environments, we plotted the growth of the several yeast strains according to number of markers belonging to the Lab Strains and the Wild Isolates.

We concluded that the presence of markers belonging to Lab Strains and Wild Isolates doesnt affect the growth. However, its ths environment itself that affects this variable (e.g.in YPD_Rapa and YPE the growth is clearly lower than the other environments, independently of the number of markers from each type).

ggplot(growth, aes(count, growth)) +
  geom_point() + geom_hex() +
  facet_grid(type~environment)
